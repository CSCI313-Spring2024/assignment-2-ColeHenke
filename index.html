Bio

Hello! My name (as you can see on the left side of the page, is Cole Henke), and I am (as you can also see on the left side of the page) a computer science major. Currently I am a junior at NDSU. I didn't start here though. During my first year of college, I attended Bismarck State College where I got my associate's degree in computer science. In my freshman year of highschool, I took a elective class that was an introductory programming course that focused on using block code in a program called "MIT App Inventor". I took the class mostly because, as a freshman, we didn't have many options for our elective classes, and I had no interst in taking an art class. To this day, it is the most fun I've ever had in a class, and it ignited a passion for solving problems using computers.

After my time at NDSU, I think I would like to end up working in a career related to machine learning or data science, but I'm not too set on that just yet. Through my past internship experience, I've learnded I also really like front-end web development. There is on thing I'm sure of though. After this semester, I vow to never even look at assembly code again. Thankfully, the chance I have to deal with it is very low, but I will avoid it like the plague.
